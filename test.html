Welcome to Corner Cottage Bed & Breakfast

Corner Cottage is an adult only Bed & Breakfast, offering a warm welcome, four well appointed en-suite bedrooms, Award Winning breakfast, guest sitting room, garden, off road parking and WiFi. There is a village pub and shop within a short walk. We are ideally located for exploring the Cotswolds and surrounding areas.

Situated on the edge of the village of Alderton on the B4077, we are within an easy drive of Tewkesbury, Winchcombe, Broadway and Cheltenham.

Rooms & Rates
