Welcome to Heskin Farm

Charlotte and Les welcome you to Heskin Farm Bed and Breakfast.

We are a small family run B&B, who look after our guests with the greatest detail. We are always happy to help.

1794 Farm House, set in rurral Lancashire, surrounded by beautiful landscapes. The farmhouse and barn have been renovated to a very good standard, and we provide plenty of car parking spaces
