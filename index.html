Welcome to Heskin Farm

Charlotte and Les offer a warm and friendly welcome at their small family run bed & breakfast in Burscough. For over 25 years, their renovated 1794 farmhouse family home, have offered plenty of parking for several cars, beautifully decorated traditional rooms, a seat in the breakfast kitchen with plenty of conversation and a good cooked breakfast Heskin Farm was awarded the highest food hygiene rating 5

Our Guests are very important to us with visitors from all over the word, returning time and time again over the years. Being described as a ‘ little gem’ by many. We may not be the best but we try our very best – for everyone

Situated in Lathom, the farmhouse is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, nearby canal walks with Burscough Village just a short few minutes’ walk away offering a good selection of Pubs and eateries.

Heskin Farm is a peaceful location off the A59 Liverpool to Preston main road with the M58 and M6 motorways in easy driving distance. Nearby are Burscough Bridge and Burscough Junction train Stations making Heskin Farm commutable to Southport, Manchester ( inc Airport ) and Preston. Liverpool being 19miles away is easy reachable from Ormskirk train station being just a few miles away.
