In the heart of the beautiful countryside of Lancashire

Attractions

Located in the beautiful countryside of Lancashire, we are surrounded by wonderful wildlife.

We are next to the Leeds to Liverpool Canal and the Rufford Branch off the main Canal. Walking and fishing are very popular here.

"Many fabulous pubs and restaurants a short walk away, returning down the pretty canal." Johnathan G - Tripadvisor

Wild Fowl Centre

The Wild Fowl Centre is one and half miles away, and the village of Burscough is only a short walk down the road

Ormskirk Market Town
